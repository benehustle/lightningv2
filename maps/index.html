Maps of the Reserve

Boundary maps, access routes and key site locations across the Lightning Ridge Crown land opal fields.

The Lightning Ridge Area Opal Reserve covers a significant area of Crown land across the Lightning Ridge opal fields in north-western New South Wales. These maps show the Reserve boundary, key access roads, mining areas, and important sites managed by the LRAOR. Click any map to view it at full size.

Acknowledgement of Country

The Lightning Ridge Area Opal Reserve acknowledges the Yuwaalaraay, Yuwaalayaay and Gamilaraay peoples as the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we work. We pay our respects to Elders past and present, and to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples who continue to care for Country.

Important Distinction

Lightning Ridge Area Opal Reserve is a delegated Crown Land Manager. Statutory approvals and regulation of mining and resources remain with Crown Lands NSW and the NSW Resources Regulator.
