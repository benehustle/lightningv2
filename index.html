Flagship project

Wandella Research & Education Hub

The Wandella farmhouse precinct has been transformed into a long-term base for visiting researchers, school groups, community programs and collaborative projects.

The next step: converting the historic Wandella shearing shed into a purpose-built education and research facility. This is the flagship project we need your help with, a permanent home for discovery, learning and innovation on Country.

Highlights from the field

Education

Wandella Education Hub growing

The Wandella farmhouse is hosting an expanding calendar of school groups and community programs, with demand growing year on year.

Environmental

600+ tubs of Hudson Pear biocontrol released

Working with NSW DPIRD and Local Land Services, we released cochineal biocontrol agents across Crown land areas of the opal fields in 2025.

Infrastructure

Grawin Front Road gazetted and upgraded

After years of advocacy, the Grawin Front Road has been formally gazetted and upgraded by Walgett Shire Council, improving access for the whole community.

Acknowledgement of Country

The Lightning Ridge Area Opal Reserve acknowledges the Yuwaalaraay, Yuwaalayaay and Gamilaraay peoples as the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we work. We pay our respects to Elders past and present, and to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples who continue to care for Country.

Important Distinction

Lightning Ridge Area Opal Reserve is a delegated Crown Land Manager. Statutory approvals and regulation of mining and resources remain with Crown Lands NSW and the NSW Resources Regulator.
